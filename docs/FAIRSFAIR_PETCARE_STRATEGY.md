# FairsFair Petcare Strategic Direction

## Overview

FairsFair Petcare is intended to become a calm operational assistance platform for pet owners, carers, walkers, groomers, and coordinators.

## Voice-Assisted Workflow Direction

Potential future capabilities:
- voice-to-text booking searches
- quick accept/reject booking flows
- hands-busy interaction support
- grooming instruction capture
- spoken instruction summarisation
- operational reminders
- voice-triggered coordination messaging

## QR Emergency Pet Profile System

Potential future system:
- QR-enabled emergency pet profiles
- owner-controlled visibility
- lost mode
- emergency handling instructions
- GDPR-aware controls

## Safety-Aware Operational Systems

Potential future assistance systems:
- weather-aware safety rules
- daylight/sunset-aware warnings
- heat alerts
- booking overlap warnings
- animal compatibility checks
